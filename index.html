Built for the Planet

Golf That's Good for the Course and the Planet

The golf industry manufactures over 1.2 billion new balls per year. Most end up lost in water hazards, forests, and roughs — taking over 100 years to decompose.

At Golf Balls Everywhere, we collect, clean, and regraded those lost balls. Every dozen you buy keeps high-quality balls in play and reduces manufacturing demand. Better golf, lighter footprint.
